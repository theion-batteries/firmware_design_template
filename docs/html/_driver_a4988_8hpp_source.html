1/*
2 a4988.cpp - - Arduino library for using the a4988 stepper driver
3 William Smith, 2014
4
5 The A4988 stepper driver is for Pololu stepper driver boards
6 and compatible clones. These boards use the Allegro a4988
7 stepper motor driver IC. (see Allegro website for datasheet)
8 This library diverges from others that are around, in that it
9 assumes that the MS1, MS2, and MS3 pins are connected to gpio
10 pins on the Arduino, allowing control over the microstepping
11 modes.
12
13 The A4988 is capable of microstepping down to 1/16 of a step,
14 enabling fine control over the stepper motor. This fine control
15 can be used in, among other things, 3D printers.
16 This library provides an interface for setting the different
17 step modes, going from full step down to 1/16 step, using a
18 simple setter function, where the argument is 1,2,4,8, or 16.
19
20 MS1 MS2 MS3
21 -----------------
22 low low low Full step
23 high low low Half step
24 low high low 1/4 step
25 high high low 1/8 step
26 high high high 1/16 step
27 Note:
28 Lower delay values can be used in the microstepping mode.
29 Values as low as 25 usec can be used in the 1/16 mode
30 with some motors.
31*/
